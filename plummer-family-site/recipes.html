Family Recipes

These are staples in our kitchen. Add your own twists and notes!

Jamaican Jerk Chicken

  • Chicken, scallions, thyme, allspice, Scotch bonnet, soy, brown sugar
  • Marinate overnight; grill until charred and cooked through

Bosnian Ćevapi

  • Ground beef/lamb, garlic, salt, pepper; shape into small sausages
  • Grill and serve with lepinja (flatbread), onions, and ajvar
